Precautions - Before and After a Volcanic Eruption


Do's Before a Volcanic Eruption

Monitor Updates:

Stay tuned to local emergency broadcasts and volcano activity updates.

Evacuation Plan:

Identify evacuation routes and shelters in advance to ensure quick evacuation.

Emergency Supplies:

Prepare a survival kit with masks, water, food, goggles, and first-aid items.

Do's After a Volcanic Eruption

Avoid Ashfall:

Stay indoors and wear masks and goggles if you must go outside.

Inspect for Damages:

Carefully check your home for ash damage or weakened structures.

Help Your Community:

Assist others in cleaning ash deposits and caring for vulnerable individuals.

Preparedness


  • Learn about the volcanoes in your area and their history.
  • Strengthen your property to withstand ashfall and minor tremors.
  • Practice evacuation drills with family and community members.
  • Establish a communication plan for emergencies.
  • Stay informed about volcanic alert levels in your region.
